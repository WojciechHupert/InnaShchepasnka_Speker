From Google to Transformation

After 10+ years at Google advising global clients, I stepped into a different kind of work. This transition wasn’t theoretical. It came through lived experience: building a startup, navigating breast cancer, and redefining what “success” means beyond linear careers.

Today, I bring that perspective into rooms where decisions are being made: MBA classrooms, founder cohorts, and leadership teams.

My work combines three things:

1

Storytelling

Making complex, emotional, or abstract topics clear and actionable for any audience.

2

Facilitation

Creating spaces where people think, speak, and engage differently than they do in standard meetings.

3

Strategic Synthesis

Turning deep insights into practical, next steps that can be implemented immediately.
